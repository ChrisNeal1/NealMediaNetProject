Our Team:

Chris Neal

Though only 26, Chris Neal has been conducting legal video depositions for over 7 years. What started with a love for video in a Sanger High class, followed by an internship with ValleyPBS while attending Fresno State, Chris intended on going into live broadcasting and video editing for a local TV station. Before being able to finish his degree, and while learning that what he wanted to do wasn't quite for him, he had the opportunity to start Neal Media Services, with a goal to provide video and media services to the Central Valley. Specializing in the legal aspect of video, Chris aims to push Neal Media toward commercial, corporate and wedding video.

On the weekends he can be found working on short films with local writers and directors throughout the Valley, and when not working on media, Chris can be found hanging out with his dog, Hennessy, and spending time with his brothers, friends and girlfriend.


Steven Bryce Jackson

Steven Jackson has always been looking for the perfect shot. Starting with a love of special effects in high school, Steven looked to grow his media skill set by attending California State University, Fresno. Where he obtained a Mass Communication and Journalism degree. After graduating he completed several independent projects before joining Neal Media Services as a Legal Video Specialist. Though he excelled in this role, Steven continued to expand his scope helping create Neal Media Services wedding and commercial video teams. He can often be found working in all aspects of a project and is not afraid to be innovative.

When Steven isn't in the office you'll find him reading, writing, or working on his next personal project. He loves to talk about his work and can't wait to hear from our clients about what amazing ideas they would like to create next.


Christy Cantu

Christy Cantu is a CSUF graduate with a BA degree in Mass Communication and Journalism. She has a breadth and depth of media experience ranging from live and taped television production and radio broadcasting to newspaper and magazine journalism. She enjoys her career as a videographer immensely and is always looking forward to learning new skills.


Raul Cantu

Raul Cantu has been working in videography professionally for over 15 years. He has extensive experience in television as a producer, director, cameraman and editor. He now enjoys a career in legal video and commercial production. In his free time, he also enjoys working on independent films in a variety of roles.
